Professional Report

At the start of this project, I wasn’t entirely sure what to expect. I had never done primary research before, and I wasn’t very familiar with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, especially Goal #4, which focuses on providing fair and equal access to quality education. Once I began learning what UNSDG #4 represents and how our topic connected to it, the project became much more clear and interesting.

My group and I chose to focus on how artificial intelligence can support equal access to education, particularly by helping students with different learning needs. According to our research, AI tools can personalize lessons, simplify materials, adjust reading levels, create translations, and provide audio support for students who struggle with language or accessibility barriers. At the same time, we learned that AI cannot solve every issue related to educational inequality—problems such as poverty, conflict, lack of devices, or limited internet access require broader social and political solutions.

Working on this project as a group taught me the importance of communication and responsibility. We had to stay organized, divide the work evenly, and meet our deadlines. The collaboration process helped me see how valuable teamwork is and allowed me to understand different perspectives as we discussed the role of AI in education.

Our survey results showed that many students use AI for explanations or tutoring, especially when they encounter difficult topics. Students also mentioned using AI to simplify information or tailor study material to their learning style. This aligned with our findings on intelligent tutoring systems—AI can analyze learning patterns, identify weaknesses, and create personalized paths that help students succeed. However, we also noted challenges such as bias, privacy concerns, device limitations, and the need for teacher training to properly integrate AI in classrooms.

Overall, this project helped me strengthen my research skills and gain a deeper understanding of how technology—especially AI—plays a growing role in modern education. I learned how to evaluate academic sources, collaborate effectively in a group, and connect research to real-world problems. More importantly, I recognized both the potential and limitations of AI in addressing global educational challenges. This experience gave me valuable insight I can apply to future academic and professional projects.
